Flight Delay Data Exploration¶

by Walid Naous¶

Investigation Overview¶

The aim of this presentation is to explore flight delay in the UD domestric air travel during the busiest month of the year 2018 - July. This presenatation will try to highlight which airline, origin airport and time of the flight may have the most delay

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset used was downloaded from http://stat-computing.org for the year 2018 and Month of July. The dataset included, flight time, cariier ID, Origin airport, planned departuret time ,Actual Departure time, delay in minutes, planned arrival time, actual arrival time

Delay duration frequency per airline¶

The investigation of carrier vs delay duration, indicate that Sky West Airlines has the longest overall delays eventhough Southwest Airline have been found to have the most number of delays, those delay are shorter compared to other airlines such as Skywest, United Airline. Moreover, the median of departure "delay" seems to be negative indicating that flights have actualy took off ahead of time.
